About Us

The Integrative Medicine Gateway, www.IMgateway.net, owned and developed by UnityHealth Pty Ltd is a unique information resource for health professionals. By collaboratively working with reputable and independent organisations, the IMgateway technology acts to disseminate quality information, research and education - to promote the integration of evidence-based complementary medicine with conventional medicine (ie. Integrative Medicine).

Sigma recognise the need for our Amcal and Guardian Pharmacists and health professionals to have efficient access to current and credible evidence-based information. This is particularly so for complementary medicines (CM) in pharmacy. Customers seek out CM advice, yet resources available for pharmacy professionals can be cumbersome, dated or insufficient. All of which can create an undesirable customer perception. Therefore, in a world first, Sigma proudly partnered with UnityHealth to co-develop our very own comprehensive health and wellbeing platform, powered by the award winning IMgateway. Sigma’s platform is designed to deliver credibility, consistency, workflow efficiency, and to facilitate professional excellence in the customer experience of CM in our retail pharmacies. Sigma’s platform is called S.H.E.R.P.A. (Sigma Holistic Evidence References & Patient Advice). A Sherpa is also a reliable and expert guide who identifies a safe path through less familiar territory. A Sherpa does the “heavy lifting” and makes it easier for the traveller too. Through S.H.E.R.P.A. Sigma’s retail pharmacies can take the leadership position in integrated medicines; confidently and safely delivering customer-centric professional advice. This reaffirms Sigma’s pursuit of excellence in our professional service-advice retail model, underpinned by our customer value proposition, and position as partner of choice for health, beauty and wellbeing.

Advertising Policy

IMgateway is committed to ensuring editorial integrity and independence. The development of our editorial content is free from advertiser bias and is not influenced by commercial interests. IMgateway will not accept advertising that is known to contain false or misleading claims. Nor will we accept advertisements that promote pornography, sexism, racism, violence, firearms, alcohol or tobacco products. The IMgateway professional databases and information can be uploaded or integrated into any third party software program.
